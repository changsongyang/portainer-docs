# How do I renew my 3 nodes free license?

If you have a 3 nodes free license that is about to expire then complete our form to request a renewal.

You'll need to provide:&#x20;

* The email that you used to signup&#x20;

Please note:&#x20;

* We will delay the sending of your new key until 14 days before your current license expiry dat&#x65;**.**

Here's the link to the form to [Request a renewal of a 3 nodes free license](https://www.portainer.io/renew-3-nodes-free-license-key?hsLang=en)

Any problems please email: [success@portainer.io](mailto:success@portainer.io)&#x20;
